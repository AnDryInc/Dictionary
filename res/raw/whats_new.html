Thanks for updating to QuickDic 3.2.

New features:

  • Go straight to last-used dictionary on launch.
  • Easier dictionary switching: long-press the switch-language button.
  • New English dictionaries: Azeri, Basque, Breton, Burmese, Faroese, Galician, Georgian, Haitian Creole, Luxembourgish, Macedonian, Tagalog, Urdu. (from enwiktionary.com data).
  • All dictionaries updated (mostly with better formatting), and can be re-downloaded at your leisure (previously downloaded dictionaries will still work).
