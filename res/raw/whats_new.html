Thanks for updating to QuickDic 3.1.

New features:

  • Search for multiple words at once (separated by spaces).
  • Long-press any word in a dictionary to lookup that word.
  • Long-press the switch-language button to switch the current dictionary.
  • New English dictionaries: Tagalog, Urdu. (from enwiktionary.com data).
  • All dictionaries updated (mostly with better formatting), and can be re-downloaded at your leisure (previously downloaded dictionaries will still work).
